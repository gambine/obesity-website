Prevention

Active prevention of cardiovascular diseases will prove to be a easier and cost effective way of combating these diseases when compared to treatment. In addition to warding off cardiovascular diseases, prevention allows the individual to live a more fulfilling life. There are more than one area of life in which the individual can actively pursure a healthier life, and within these areas, there are a multitude of options to choose from.
