Help/FAQ

What is our purpose to create this website?

The purpose is to develop an interactive website that provides quality information to the public about cardiovascular disease and presents this information in a way that is appealing to the user, encouraging them to return and use the website again. The website will allow the public to become more aware of the causes and effects of cardiovascular disease, the treatment and prevention, relevant statistics and useful links. The website will also provide tools to allow users to better understand their current health conditions. Users will be able to customize the website according to their profile, allowing them to change the theme, colour and the information to be displayed.

What is BMI

Body mass index (BMI) is a measure of body fat based on your weight in relation to your height, and applies to most adult men and women aged 20 and over. For children aged 2 and over, BMI percentile is the best assessment of body fat.

How does the BMI calculator work?

It is used to calculate your BMI(Body Mass Index).First, users should input their weight(kg) and height(m). Then the calculator will the formula BMI = Weight(kg) / (Height(m) * Height(m))to get the correct number for the user.

How can this information be used to improve my lifestyle

The BMI is an attempt to quantify the amount of tissue mass (muscle, fat, and bone) in an individual, and then categorize that person as underweight, normal weight, overweight, or obese based on that value. Commonly accepted BMI ranges are underweight: under 18.5, normal weight: 18.5 to 25, overweight: 25 to 30, obese: over 30. It is a good method to let people know their body status and help them to improve their lifestyle.
