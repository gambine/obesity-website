Caesar Salad

You'll love this healthier version of a classic salad
​Serves 4
Preparation time: 15 minutes
Cooking time: 10 minutes
Ingredients
​Dressing:
1 tbs white wine vinegar
1 tbs lemon juice
2 tbs water
½ tsp Dijon mustard
2 cloves garlic, peeled and crushed
1 egg yolk
2 anchovy fillets, patted dry of excess oil and very finely chopped 
Black pepper to season

Salad:
3 thick slices bread*
2 baby cos lettuces, cut in quarters
4 soft boiled eggs*, peeled and cut in half
200g small tomatoes, chopped
2 tbs finely grated Parmesan

*Products available with the Heart Foundation Tick. 
Remember all fresh fruit and vegetables automatically qualify for the Tick.
​1. Whisk together all the dressing ingredients, season with pepper and refrigerate for 15 minutes.

2. Cut bread into cubes or roughly tear into small pieces. 
Place on baking tray and place under preheated grill. 
Grill until golden brown. Set aside to cool.

3. Arrange cos, eggs, tomatoes and bread in serving bowls, drizzle over dressing and sprinkle with Parmesan.
