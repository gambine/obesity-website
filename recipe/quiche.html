Ham, leek and swiss cheese quiche

Make meal time fun with this creative quiche!
​Serves 6
Preparation time: 15 minutes
cooking time: 30 minutes
Ingredients
​Quiche:
8 eggs
1 sheet Tick approved puff pastry
1 leek, pale part only, washed well and thinly sliced
120g salt reduced lean  ham, shaved and diced
120g grated reduced fat Swiss cheese
200ml reduced fat milk
Pinch of ground nutmeg
Pepper to taste
Canola spray oil

The face:
1 egg, hard boiled and cut in half
1 cherry tomato cut in half
6 asparagus spears, cut in half
¼ avocado
1 thick cucumber slice
​1. Pre heat oven to 180°C.

2. Grease a 23cm quiche tin with spray oil, line with pastry sheet and trim edges.

3. Cover with a sheet of non-stick baking paper, fill with baking weights and place into the oven
 until blind baked (without colouration).

4. Heat a frying pan on gentle heat, spray with oil and add leek. Gently cook without colour until soft.

5. In a bowl, whisk together the milk, eggs, nutmeg and pepper.

6. Once the pastry is blind baked, remove from oven and take out the paper and weights.
 Fill with leeks, ham and egg mixture, and top with cheese.

7. Place back into the oven for 30 minutes or until filling is just set.

8. Remove quiche from oven and garnish with eggs as eyes, tomato as cheeks, asparagus as hair,
 avocado as the mouth and cucumber as the nose.

Tip: Serve with a large side salad for everyone to share and salt reduced tomato sauce.
