Your estimated BMR is...


Your Basal Metabolic Rate (BMR) is an estimate of how many calories your body burns at rest. It represents the minimum amount of energy needed to keep your body functioning, including breathing and keeping your heart beating. Your BMR uses up about two-thirds of your daily calories. Your caloric intake to lose, maintain, or gain weight will be based on your BMR, but will not be the same figure.
